Qualitative approaches: Introduction to the course

QUALITATIVE APPROACH TO RESEARCH

By attending this course, students will:

●Get familiar with the fundamental elements of a qualitative approach to research,
●Improve the methods of data collection and data analysis,
●Become proficient in the qualitative methods
●Be able to carry out an in-depth treatment of findings.

Course objectives:

At the end of this course students will :

Understand nature of qualitative methods or approaches,

Know the characteristics of qualitative research method,

Know the various approaches to the conduct of qualitative research,

Make the difference between qualitative and quantitative methods,

Be able to conduct a research adopting the  qualitative approach.
